# Minnesota Portage

An educational adventure for ages **9–11**. Carry a birchbark **Story Bundle** along Minnesota’s water roads, gather **Story Stones**, and arrive at the **Council of Stories**.

A *portage* is carrying a canoe over land between lakes — here you also carry living knowledge with care.

## Play locally

```bash
cd "Minnesota Trail"
python3 -m http.server 8080
```

Then open [http://localhost:8080](http://localhost:8080).

## Your quest

1. Form a crew (1–10 hotseat) and pick difficulty (Medium recommended).
2. Meet your trail companion.
3. At each stop: learn, play, hunt/forage, or answer history questions.
4. Earn Story Stones for the Bundle.
5. Keep health, energy, and food up; cure ailments; wear outfits that change your look.
6. Reach the Council and open the Bundle — do well for a **Portage Party**!

Tap 🔊 chips to hear pronunciations (manoomin, Boozhoo, Bdote, Gichi-gami, and more).

## Respect

Dakota and Ojibwe cultures are living cultures. Sacred places are for protection and learning. The dig site is a **practice** excavation only.
