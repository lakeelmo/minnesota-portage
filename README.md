# Minnesota Portage

An educational **board game** for ages **9–11**. Carry a birchbark **Story Bundle** along the St. Croix valley, earn **Story Stones**, and reach the **Council of Stories**.

A *portage* is carrying a canoe over land between lakes — here you also carry living knowledge with care.

## Play locally

```bash
cd "Minnesota Trail"
python3 -m http.server 8080
```

Then open [http://localhost:8080](http://localhost:8080). Best in **landscape** on a phone; fullscreen on a computer.

## How to play

1. Pick players (1–4 hotseat) and difficulty — **no typing names**; choose a valley traveler.
2. Each turn: **roll the die**, move exactly that many spaces (branches = strategy).
3. Land on a space → **Story Card** (deep trivia, no repeats), **Challenge** minigame, Camp, or Hazard.
4. Gather enough Story Stones, then reach the **Council of Stories**.

## Respect

Dakota and Ojibwe cultures are living cultures. Sacred places are for protection and learning. The dig site is a **practice** excavation only.
