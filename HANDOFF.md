## Current design (race)

- Left→right valley board (~24 spaces). First to **Council** wins.
- Die faces: **1 / 2 / 3 / 🎮** (challenge).
- Every landing requires a **Story Card** to stay; miss → bounce back.
- Solo games add a **CPU rival** (you see their Q&A).
- Questions: **answers left · question right**.
- Minigames: **fullscreen**; arrows + mouse/click.
- No Story Stones.
